## Quick Demo

### 1. Create an Account (First Time Only)
- Click **Account** (top right)
- Click **"Register here"**
- Enter any username, email, name, and password
- Click **Register**
- You'll be redirected to login

### 2. Login
- Enter your credentials
- Click **Login**

### 3. Browse Products
- See products on the home page
- Click on any product to view details
- Use categories to filter
- Use sidebar filters for price, rating, sort

### 4. Add to Cart
- Click **"Add to Cart"** on any product
- Quantity will appear in the cart badge 🛒

### 5. Checkout
- Click **Cart** icon
- Review items
- Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
- Enter shipping address
- Select payment method
- Click **"Place Order"**

### 6. View Orders
- Click **Account**
- Go to **"My Orders"** tab
- See your order history

## Customization

Want to add more products?
- Edit the `insertSampleProducts()` function in `server.js`
- Add product details (name, price, category, discount)
- Restart the server

Want to change colors?
- Edit `public/styles.css`
- Modify the CSS custom properties (--primary-color, etc.)
